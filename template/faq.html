Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions that we get asked. If you don't see your question answered here, please don't hesitate to contact us.

  • What kind of cars do you import?

    We import all kinds of cars from Dubai, including luxury cars, sports cars, and everyday vehicles.

  • How long does the import process take?

    The import process typically takes between 4-6 weeks from the time that we receive your order.

  • What is the cost of importing a car?

    The cost of importing a car can vary depending on a number of factors such as the make and model of the car, its condition, and the shipping costs.

  • Do you handle all the paperwork?

    Yes, we take care of all the necessary paperwork and ensure that everything is done correctly and efficiently.

  • How do I pay for my car?

    We accept payment via bank transfer or credit card.
